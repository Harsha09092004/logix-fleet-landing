## 📊 **Executive Overview**

LinkedIn is the #1 platform for B2B decision-maker engagement in Indian logistics. Our strategy targets:
- **Operations Managers** (1,500+ hrs/year in invoice processing)
- **Finance Directors** (managing $500K-$5M AP budgets)
- **Founders/CEOs** (ROI-focused, early adopters)

**Primary Goal:** Generate 200+ qualified leads in 90 days with 8-12% conversion to free trial signup.

**Daily Connection Quota:** 50 personalized connections
- 20% Tier 1 decision-makers
- 50% Tier 2 operations/finance leads
- 30% Relevant connectors (consultants, logistics tech founders)

**Connection Message Template:**

```

invoice processing time from 40 minutes to 12 minutes using AI automation.

Curious if {{CompanyName}} faces similar challenges with manual invoice entry?

I'm not selling anything – just sharing what worked for similar companies.
```

**Personalization Rules:**
- Always mention their company name
- Reference their recent post/activity if available

## 📈 **Phase 7: Metrics & Optimization**

### Weekly Tracking
- **Connection request sent:** Target 250/week
- **Acceptance rate:** Target 35-40%
- **Message open rate:** Target 60%+
- **Reply rate:** Target 15-20%
- **Meeting booked:** Target 3-5/week
- **Demo conversion:** Target 8-12%
- **Free trial signup:** Target 1-2/week

### Monthly KPIs
- **Leads generated:** 50+
- **Cost per lead:** ₹500-1000 (internal time)
- **Conversion to trial:** 8-12%
- **Trial to customer:** 30-40%

### A/B Testing Framework
**Test 1 (Week 1-2):** Connection message variant A vs B
- A: Problem-first ("We found...")
- B: Personal-first ("I noticed...")
- Winner: Use for next 2 weeks

**Test 2 (Week 3-4):** Follow-up message variant A vs B
- A: Short (1 paragraph)
- B: Long (3 paragraphs with data)
- Winner: Use for rest of campaign

**Test 3 (Week 5-6):** CTA variant A vs B
- A: Direct: "Want a demo?"
- B: Soft: "Should we talk?"
- Winner: Use in all future outreach
