## PRO TIPS

1. **Personal > Corporate**: Post from YOUR personal account, not company account (better reach)
2. **Reply > Ignore**: Set phone reminder to check comments every hour on post day
3. **Ask questions**: "What's your biggest invoice pain?" = More engagement
4. **Use emojis**: 🚚📊💰 = Better click-through rates
5. **Short sentences**: Modern readers scan, don't read paragraphs
6. **CTA clear**: Every post should have one clear call-to-action [LINK] or DM
7. **Tag people**: @Real people (not spam accounts) = more visibility
