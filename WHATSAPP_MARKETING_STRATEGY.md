## 📱 **Executive Overview**

WhatsApp is the #1 messaging platform in India with 500M+ users. For B2B logistics decision-makers:
- **50M+** business users on WhatsApp Business
- **6x higher** open rate vs email (65% vs 11%)

**Primary Goal:** Convert LinkedIn connections into WhatsApp leads, then nurture to free trial signup with 40-50% conversion rate.

1. Go to: https://www.whatsapp.com/business/
2. Click "Get Started"
3. Sign in with Meta Business Account
4. Provide:
   - Business name: "FreightFlow"
   - Business address: India (Bangalore preferred)
   - Phone number: +91-XXXXXXXXX (dedicated number)
   - Use case: B2B invoice automation

**Step 2: Get WhatsApp Business Phone Number**
- Dedicated phone number (not personal)
- Cost: ₹0 (first month free), then ₹0.002 per message (variable cost)
- Recommended: Get 2 numbers for redundancy

**Step 3: Create Business Profile**
- Profile name: "FreightFlow"
- Description: "AI Invoice Automation for Logistics"
- Profile picture: FreightFlow logo
- CTA button: "Start Free Trial" or "Book Demo"

All WhatsApp messages must follow Meta's message template system. Templates get pre-approved to ensure compliance.
