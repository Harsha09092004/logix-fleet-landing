**Goal**: Acquire 100 paying customers across 3 customer segments

**Timeline**: 6 months (April - September 2026)

- Week 4-6: ₹12L (Rate Card feature launch + sales team expansion)
- Week 7-10: ₹15L (POD + Geofencing feature launch + enterprise pilot)

**Revenue Projection**:

- June-July: ₹25-35L (30-40 customers)
- August-September: ₹50-65L (80-100 customers)

## PART 1: CUSTOMER SEGMENTATION

### Segment 1: SME Shippers (40% of target)
**Profile**: 
- 10-50 shipments/month
- Manual invoice entry, basic spreadsheet tracking
- 2-5 logistics staff
- Budget: ₹30K-50K/month
- Pain point: Invoice data entry is 5-10 person-hours/week

**Companies**: Specialized traders, small manufacturers, e-commerce resellers

**Acquisition Channel**: 
- LinkedIn direct outreach (100 top SMEs)
- Email campaigns (shipper databases)
- WhatsApp broadcast to logistics park communities
- Free trial: 30-day unlimited usage, cancel anytime

### Segment 2: Mid-Market 3PL & Fleet Operators (40% of target)
**Profile**:
- 500-5000 shipments/month
- Have basic TMS but limited automation
- 10-30 logistics staff
- Budget: ₹50K-150K/month
- Pain point: Route optimization, driver accountability, invoice reconciliation

**Companies**: Regional logistics companies, captive fleet operators

**Acquisition Channel**:
- Trade shows (Logistics Show India, Fleet & Transport Summit)
- Industry associations (3PLSA, TCI, Allcargo networks)
- Direct sales team (2-3 enterprise sales reps)
- POD + Geofencing pilot program: 3-month free trial

### Segment 3: Enterprise Logistics Networks (20% of target)
**Profile**:
- 10K+ shipments/month
- Use enterprise TMS (JiTLogix, Locus, etc.) but need SME-friendly alternatives
- 50+ staff
- Budget: ₹200K-500K/month (custom pricing)
- Pain point: High licensing costs, vendor lock-in, limited flexibility

**Companies**: Large 3PLs, national courier networks

**Acquisition Channel**:
- Executive roundtables + advisory board meetings
- Investor pitch + case studies
- POC (Proof of Concept) program: Migrate 1 hub + 100 shipments free
- Partnership with franchise networks

## PART 2: WEEK-BY-WEEK ACQUISITION ROADMAP

### PHASE 1: OCR Launch (Week 1-3) — 10-15 Customers

#### Week 1: Soft Launch + Early Adopters
**What**: Release OCR invoice capture to beta users
**Target**: 10 highly technical SMEs who'll become advocates

**Actions**:
1. Email beta testers (from earlier communication)
   - Subject: "Save 10 hours/week: Automated invoice data extraction (Beta)"
   - CTA: "Get early access + 3-month free OCR"
   - Expected response: 30-50 opens → 8-12 signups

2. LinkedIn post: "We just built OCR for Indian freight invoices"
   - Showcase: Before/After screenshots of TCI/Allcargo/Delhivery invoices
   - Engagement: Tag 20 logistics influencers
   - Expected: 50+ applications

3. WhatsApp message to logistics parks
   - Partner with 3-4 logistics parks (Nerul, Chakan, Kothrud)
   - 500 WA messages: "Free OCR pilot for trucking companies"
   - Expected: 15-20 sign-ups

4. Customer Success alignment
   - Assign dedicated CSM to each early customer
   - Daily check-ins for 2 weeks
   - Document success stories for case studies

**Metrics to Track**:
- Sign-ups: Target 50
- Free-to-trial conversion: 60-70%
- Trial-to-paid conversion: 15-20%
- Expected paying customers: 8-12

#### Week 2-3: Early Advocate Program
**What**: Convert 60% of free users to paid + get referrals

**Actions**:
1. Webinar: "How TCI Invoice OCR Saved 3 Companies 40 Hours/Week"
   - Invite: 200 SMEs via LinkedIn message
   - Speakers: 2 early customers + CEO
   - Expected attendance: 50-80
   - Expected conversions: 5-8

2. Direct sales outreach
   - Sales team (you or 1 founder) calls top 50 free users
   - Offer: 40% discount if they commit to 6-month annual plan
   - Expected: 3-5 conversions

3. Referral program launch
   - Offer: ₹1000 Amazon gift card per referred customer who pays
   - Target: Get 3-5 referrals from early adopters

4. Case study + testimonial collection
   - Reach out to 8 early paid customers
   - Request 15-minute testimonial video
   - Offer: 20% discount extension for cooperating

**Metrics to Track**:
- Free users retained: 40-50
- Paid conversions: 5-8
- Referrals generated: 3-5
- Total paying customers: 13-25
