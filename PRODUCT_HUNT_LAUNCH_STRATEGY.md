## 📅 **Phase 1: Launch Timing & Preparation (Week 1-2)**

### Optimal Launch Timing

**Best Day:** Tuesday or Wednesday (not Monday or Friday)
- Monday: Low engagement (weekend recovery)
- Tuesday-Wednesday: Peak engagement
- Thursday-Friday: People checking out for weekend
- Weekend: Very low engagement

**Best Time:** 12:01 AM PST (Pacific) = 12:31 PM IST (India)
- Reason: First in line, accumulates momentum all day
- Gets the entire US business day + Asia evening
- By end of day PST, has full 24-hour upvote advantage

**Chosen:** Tuesday, June 2, 2026 at 12:01 AM PST

- 0.3% error rate

ROI: ₹500K/month

## Getting Started

## 💡 **Phase 7: Common Mistakes to Avoid**

❌ **Mistake 1: Publishing too late**
- Don't wait for "perfect"
- Publish 48-72 hours before launch
- Small tweaks OK, but not major changes Day-of

❌ **Mistake 2: Not being responsive**
- Comments = customers engaging
- 5-minute response time is bare minimum
- 15+ minute lag = risk of low rank

❌ **Mistake 3: Ignoring criticism**
- Defensive replies kill momentum
- Respond to concerns thoughtfully
- "That's a fair point – here's how we address it"

❌ **Mistake 4: Over-promoting**
- Don't spam with "upvote me"
- Focus on value, not asks
- Let product speak for itself

❌ **Mistake 5: Launching tired**
- Don't pull all-nighter Day-of
- Be sharp, energized, ready to engage
- Delegate if you can't be present
