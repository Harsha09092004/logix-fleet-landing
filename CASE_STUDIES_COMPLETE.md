## CASE STUDY 1: SMALL SME - EXPRESS LOGISTICS PRIVATE LTD

### Company Profile
**Logo**: 🚛  
**Company**: Express Logistics Private Ltd  
**Headquarters**: Mumbai, Maharashtra  
**Business**: Regional logistics for food & FMCG companies  
**Employees**: 25-30  
**Annual Turnover**: ₹50 Lakh (FY 2025)  
**Invoice Volume**: 150-200 invoices/month  

---

### The Problem

**Before FreightFlow**:
- Manual invoice entry: 4-5 days/week of work
- One person (accountant) doing all invoice processing
- Frequent GST calculation errors (7-10% error rate)
- Monthly reconciliation took 3-4 days
- Vendor disputes over invoice amounts
- No vendor tracking or audit trail

**Pain Points**:
- ₹3 Lakh/year in accountant salary wasted on manual data entry
- 3-4 invoices processed incorrectly per day (6-8% error rate)
- Delayed GST filing due to errors and rework
- No visibility into vendor trends or performance

---

### FreightFlow Implementation

**Timeline**: 2 weeks (including onboarding)
- Week 1: Setup, team training, first 20 invoices processed
- Week 2: Full rollout, process optimization, monitoring

**Setup Effort**: 
- Admin: 2 hours (user onboarding, settings)
- Accountant training: 4 hours
- OCR fine-tuning: 1 hour
- **Total**: ~7 hours

---

### Results After 3 Months

| Metric | Before | After | Change |
|--------|--------|-------|--------|
| **Processing Time** | 30-40 min/invoice | 3-5 sec auto + 1 min review | 85% reduction |
| **Monthly Processing Hours** | 100-120 hrs | 15-20 hrs | 5-6x faster |
| **Error Rate** | 7-10% | 0.5-1% | 90% reduction |
| **GST Reconciliation Time** | 3-4 days | 15-30 minutes | 95% reduction |
| **Vendor Accuracy** | 70-80% | 99.5% | 25%+ improvement |
| **Monthly Cost Savings** | - | ₹25,000 | Annual: ₹3 Lakh |

---

### ROI Calculation

**Investment**:
- Software (Pro plan): ₹15,000/month
- Training & setup: 7 hours @ ₹500/hr = ₹3,500
- **Monthly Cost**: ₹15,000 + (~₹1,500 ongoing setup)

**Savings**:
- Accountant time freed up: 80-100 hrs/month
- Hourly rate equivalent: ₹500/hour
- **Monthly Savings**: ₹40,000-50,000
- **Labor efficiency**: ₹25,000-30,000 net savings after software

**Payback Period**: 
- Total initial cost: ₹3,500
- Monthly net savings: ₹25,000
- **Payback**: 1 week (0.2 months)

**Annual ROI**: 
- Annual savings: ₹3 Lakh (labor) + ₹5,000 (reduced errors)
- Annual cost: ₹1.8 Lakh (software)
- **Net annual benefit**: ₹1.35 Lakh (75% ROI)

---

### Customer Quote

## CASE STUDY 2: MID-SIZE OPERATOR - TCI LOGISTICS BRANCH

### Company Profile
**Logo**: 🚛🚛🚛  
**Company**: TCI Express - Mumbai Branch  
**Headquarters**: Mumbai (part of ₹500 Cr+ national network)  
**Business**: Pan-India freight & LTL services  
**Employees**: 50-80 at branch level  
**Local Annual Turnover**: ₹5 Crore  
**Invoice Volume**: 800-1200 invoices/month  

---

### The Problem

**Before FreightFlow**:
- Finance team of 3-4 people processing invoices
- 45-50% of invoices had errors needing rework
- Manual GST calculation for every invoice (prone to mistakes)
- Reverse charge (RCM) invoices calculated incorrectly
- Monthly reconciliation: 5-6 days of work across team
- Vendor complaints about invoice accuracy
- No real-time visibility into daily invoice volume or GST liability

**Pain Points**:
- ₹15-20 Lakh/year in Finance salary allocated to data entry

- Lack of actionable vendor analytics
- Manual spreadsheet tracking = high risk

---

**Timeline**: 3 weeks (multi-user rollout)
- Week 1: Setup for 2 power users (finance leads)
- Week 2: Extended to full finance team (3-4 users)

- Admin setup: 3 hours
- Finance team training: 8 hours (4 users × 2 hrs each)

**License**: Pro plan (500 invoices/month) upgraded to Enterprise for ₹35,000/month

**Invoice Volume**: 3000-4000 invoices/month  

---

### The Problem

**Before FreightFlow**:
- Finance team of 8-10 people processing invoices
- Distributed across 3 sub-teams (Mumbai, Delhi, Bangalore)
- Inconsistent invoice processing across regions
- 8-10% error rate despite team effort
