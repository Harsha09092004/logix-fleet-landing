# FreightFlow: Referral Program Strategy

**Phase:** 2.4 | **Status:** Ready for Launch | **Target:** 100+ referrals in 90 days

---

## 🎯 **Executive Overview**

Referral is the highest-converting, lowest-cost acquisition channel for B2B SaaS:
- **Cost per acquisition:** ₹1000-2000 (vs ₹5000-8000 for paid ads)
- **Conversion rate:** 25-30% (vs 2-5% for cold outreach)
- **Customer lifetime value:** 16-25% higher
- **Churn rate:** 7% lower

**Primary Goal:** Turn happy customers into brand advocates who bring 3-5 new customers each in 90 days.

---

## 💰 **Phase 1: Referral Incentive Structure**

### Tier 1: Early Adopters (Month 1-3)

**For Referrer (Existing Customer):**
- Referral credit: **₹5,000 per qualified referral**
- How to claim: "Customer signs up for paid plan"
- Max credits: ₹50K/month (10 referrals)
- Format: Credit on next invoice OR cashback to bank account

**For Referee (New Customer):**
- Sign-up bonus: **₹2,500 free credits**
- Duration: Applied to first month's bill
- Requirement: Active 7+ days in trial
- No credit card required to claim

**Example:**
```
Reliance Operations Manager refers TCS Finance Director:
✓ Reliance gets ₹5,000 credit
✓ TCS gets ₹2,500 credit + 14-day free trial
✓ Both win!
```

---

### Tier 2: Scaling (Month 4-6)

**For Referrer:**
- First 5 referrals: ₹5,000 each
- 6-10 referrals: ₹7,500 each (10% bonus)
- 11+ referrals: ₹10,000 each (20% bonus)
- Max credits: Unlimited
- Tiered bonus: If you hit 10 referrals/month → 20% loyalty bonus

**For Referee:**
- Standard: ₹2,500 credit
- If referred by top advocate: ₹5,000 credit (matched referrer's bonus)

- Credit shows in dashboard and invoice immediately

**Status 6: Active Customer**
- Recipient maintaining paid subscription

## 🎁 **Phase 5: Referral Rewards Options**

### Option 1: Account Credits
- Monthly bill reduced by referral credit amount
- Simplest for customers already on paid plans
- Example: ₹50K/month bill - ₹15K referral credits = ₹35K due

### Option 2: Cash Rewards
- Direct bank transfer to referrer's account
- 15% processing fee (₹5,000 → ₹4,250 payout)
- Requires: Bank account verification, PAN card
- Processing time: 3-5 business days

### Option 3: Account Add-ons
- ₹5,000 referral → $100 worth of premium features
- Extra vendor accounts
- Advanced reporting modules
- Priority support tier

### Option 4: Combo (Recommended)
- 60% as account credit (automatic, instant)
- 40% as cash reward (upon request)
- Example: ₹5,000 → ₹3,000 credit + ₹2,000 cash

## 🛠️ **Phase 9: System Integration Requirements**

### What We Need to Build

1. **Referral Link Generator**
   - Unique code per customer: {{CUSTOMER_ID}}_{{RANDOM_CODE}}
   - Trackable in database
   - Persistent across sessions

2. **Referral Landing Page**
   - Dynamic: Shows referrer name at top
   - Message: "{{RefererName}} thinks you should try this"
   - CTA: Pre-filled email/company info from link
   - Copy: Emphasize referrer's recommendation

3. **Referral Dashboard Widget**
   - Show referral link with copy button
   - Show referral history (who you referred)
   - Show earning progress
   - Track status of pending referrals

4. **Referral Tracking Backend**
   - Log when link clicked
   - Log when trial starts
   - Log when upgrade happens
   - Trigger credit issuance

5. **Notification System**
   - Notify referrer when someone clicks link
   - Notify referrer when referral starts trial
   - Notify referrer when referral upgrades
   - Notify referrer when credit applied

6. **Payment Integration**
   - Deduct referral credits from monthly invoices
   - Bank transfer payout system (if cash option)
